Safety Rules for Secure Shipping

  • Verify all documents and licenses before accepting a shipment or bid.
  • Ensure cargo is properly packed and labeled for safe transport.
  • Use only registered and insured vehicles for shipping.
  • Share real-time tracking and status updates with all parties.
  • Never share sensitive information outside the platform.
  • Report any suspicious activity to Safe Haul support immediately.
